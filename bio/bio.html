My name is Kelly Hodges and I am currently working through the Software Engineering Immersive Program at General Assembly.

I love to learn and believe it is healthy to keep learning and challenging yourself throughout your life. Luckily, there is a lot of learning to be had when it comes to software engineering.

I was born and raised in Long Beach, CA. I lived in San Francisco for a year, New Orleans for 6 years, and now I am back in good old Long Beach.

I have an extreme love for baking and chomping pizza. I have a library of photos of the creative pizzas that my husband and I make, scroll down to see a few.
